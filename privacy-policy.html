Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Overview

Kodek Media ("we," "us," or "our") is a TikTok brand empowerment agency. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when visiting our website or submitting an application through our forms.

By using our website or submitting an application, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website or submit your information.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Ever.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you complete one of our application forms (Brand & Seller Inquiry, LIVE Creator Program, or TikTok Shop Affiliate), we may collect:

  • Full name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Business or brand name and website URL
  • TikTok handle, profile URL, and account metrics (followers, views, GMV)
  • Content niche, product categories, and business details
  • Goals, challenges, and other information you choose to share in open-text fields

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect standard technical data including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring URLs and general geographic region (country/city)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile)

This data is collected through standard web analytics tools and is used in aggregate form only.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide exclusively to:

  • Review and respond to your application or inquiry
  • Contact you to discuss potential opportunities or next steps
  • Understand your needs so we can offer relevant services
  • Send you updates about Kodek Media programs (only where you have opted in)
  • Improve our website and application process

We will never use your information for purposes unrelated to your application or relationship with Kodek Media without your explicit consent.

How We Store and Protect Your Data

Your information is stored securely and accessible only to authorized Kodek Media team members who need it to process your application. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

We retain your application data for up to 24 months. If you would like your data deleted sooner, you may request this at any time (see "Your Rights" below).

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We may use trusted tools (e.g., form processing, email services) that handle your data on our behalf, bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of Kodek Media or others.

Any third-party tools we use are carefully vetted and contractually obligated to protect your data.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies for basic analytics (e.g., understanding which pages are most visited). These are non-advertising, non-tracking cookies.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to view our website or submit an application.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to TikTok or other third-party platforms. This Privacy Policy applies only to the Kodek Media website. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party sites, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information through our website, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Request that we delete your information
  • Opt out of any marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@kodekmedia.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
